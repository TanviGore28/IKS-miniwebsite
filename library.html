1. Uses of Library Documents, Books, Vedas etc. in Data Science:

  • Source of Data- Provide authentic textual material that can be digitized and converted into datasets.
  • Training AI Models- Useful for natural language processing (NLP), machine translation, and semantic analysis.
  • Pattern & Trend Analysis- Helps students identify how knowledge, language, and culture evolved across time.
  • Knowledge Integration- Ancient texts (like Vedas) can be compared with modern research for interdisciplinary studies.
  • Educational Practice- Give students real-world data for learning OCR, text mining, and machine learning techniques.


    • 2.Preserving Library Content through Data Science:

  • Digitization- Scanning and converting books, documents, and manuscripts into digital form.
  • OCR Technology- Transforming printed or handwritten texts into machine-readable data.
  • Metadata Creation- Tagging title, author, subject, and keywords for easy search and retrieval.
  • Cloud & Database Storage- Ensuring safe preservation and wide accessibility.
  • AI-based Restoration- Enhancing faded texts, restoring damaged pages, and preserving authenticity.
  • Digital Archives- Building online repositories to make content available for future generations.
